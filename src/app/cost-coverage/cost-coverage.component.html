Telehealth in Psychiatry

Telehealth has proven to be an important way for patients to access medical services that may be otherwise out of reach for millions of people. We live busy lives and it is often difficult to make time for self-care. Additionally, the fact is that there is unfortunately a stigma that many of us face when dealing with our mental health. For these reasons, up to 60% of patients who access psychiatric/psychological treatment are accessing this type of care for the first time ever. We offer a private, secure way to access care without having to take hours out of your busy schedule.
